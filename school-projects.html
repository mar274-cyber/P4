School Projects

Data Analytics Project I

This project analyzes online customer behavior for J&J Chocolates, a New Jersey–based chocolate manufacturer that recently expanded into e-commerce. After online sales exceeded expectations, the company selected a sample of 50 website transactions to better understand customer activity. The dataset includes the day of purchase, payment method, time spent on the website, number of pages viewed, and the dollar amount spent.

The goal of the project is to use descriptive statistics and visual analysis to determine how customer behaviors—such as time on site and pages viewed—relate to spending. The project also evaluates how day of the week and payment method affect sales performance. Based on the insights from graphs, summary statistics, and correlation analysis, the final report provides data-driven recommendations to help J&J Chocolates increase online sales.

Data Analytics Project II

This project focuses on forecasting monthly sales for Joe’s Stone Crab, a historic and nationally renowned seafood restaurant in Miami Beach. Using monthly sales data from 2020–2023, we built a 2024 sales forecast using a time-series seasonal adjustment model.

The project required identifying trends, seasonal patterns, and future expectations, then presenting the results in a clear, business-friendly format for the restaurant’s owner. Deliverables included the 2024 monthly forecast, seasonal factors, trend calculations, error measures, and visualizations.
